About ZION

PALIMPSEST

Welcome to ZION 2026, the ultimate confluence of technology and culture. This year's theme, "PALIMPSEST - Rewrite the Future", challenges us to build upon the foundations of the past to create an innovative and dynamic tomorrow.

Join thousands of students, innovators, and creators in a 3-day extravaganza featuring technical hackathons, cultural performances, interactive workshops, and gaming tournaments.
